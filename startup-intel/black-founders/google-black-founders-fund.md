# Google Black Founders Fund

## Summary
Integration strategy for leveraging the Google for Startups Black Founders Fund.

## Purpose
To acquire non-dilutive capital, hands-on support, and extensive Cloud credits to propel the Maroon OS infrastructure.

## System Integration
- **Master Agent Routing**: The Master Agent tracks system milestones required for application and elevation.
- **Credit Deployment Plan**: Credits acquired through this fund are routed into the 'Wave 2' and 'Wave 3' scaling phases, providing up to $100k+ in non-dilutive capital and cloud credits.
- **Reporting**: BigQuery stores tracking metrics for fund compliance and progress reporting.\n
